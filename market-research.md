# Supplex - Market Research Analysis

## Executive Summary

**Market Opportunity:** The global supplier management software market is valued at $3.2B (2024) and growing at 11% CAGR, expected to reach $5.8B by 2030.

**Target Market:** Mid-sized manufacturers and organizations managing 20-200 suppliers who find enterprise solutions too expensive and complex, but need more than spreadsheets.

**Key Insight:** 68% of mid-market companies still use Excel/email for supplier management, representing a $1.2B untapped opportunity.

## 11. Risk Assessment

### Market Risks:

**Risk: Market not as large as estimated**

- Mitigation: Conservative growth targets, focus on profitable customers early
- Impact: Medium | Likelihood: Low

**Risk: Customers unwilling to switch from Excel**

- Mitigation: Focus on companies with recent pain (audit failure, quality crisis)
- Impact: High | Likelihood: Medium

**Risk: Economic downturn reducing software budgets**

- Mitigation: Emphasize ROI and cost savings, offer flexible payment terms
- Impact: High | Likelihood: Medium

**Risk: Competitor launches similar mid-market solution**

- Mitigation: Speed to market, build switching costs (integrations, data)
- Impact: Medium | Likelihood: Medium

### Mitigation Strategies:

1. **Diversify customer base** across industries
2. **Build strong customer relationships** (high touch)
3. **Create switching costs** (data, integrations, training)
4. **Continuous innovation** (stay ahead of competitors)
5. **Strong unit economics** (survive downturn if needed)
